Duke


Duke is a task manager. Duke can record your tasks in your daliy life. With Duke, you will never forget important tasks. Duke can manage you task well!

Features:

  • Home page
  • Store and display stall information
  • Display stall information and menus based on current system date time
  • Calculate estimated waiting time by the number of people in the queue the user enters
  • Allow to check the operating hours for all stalls
