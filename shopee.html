Shopee

Backend Engineer Intern (Jul 2021 - Present)
Data Engineer Intern (May 2021 - Jul 2021)

Shopee was founded in Singapore in 2015 and has since grown to become a prominent e-commerce platform in Southeast Asia and Taiwan, offering customers a simple, secure, and convenient online shopping experience.

During my first 12 weeks of full-time internship as a Data Engineer Intern, I was primarily responsible for developing Spark data pipelines on distributed systems, and I programmed in Scala on Hadoop framework (HDFS, Spark, Yarn) and scheduled using Airflow. Beginning with the entry assignment, I am now capable of designing and completing a data pipeline project on my own.

After working full-time for Shopee for 12 weeks and gaining a comprehensive view of the data engineering area, I'd want to try something new and steepen my learning curve once again. As a result, I sought a transfer to work part-time as a Backend Engineer Intern during the semester. I'm still on my backend journey to develop some APIs using Golang. I'll write more about it here as I get more experience.

This has been a fruitful internship so far that allows me to get exposure to an area rarely explicitly taught in school. This experience would provide me with a clearer picture while making decisions about my future career path and, most importantly, being more adaptable and resilient to the challenges I may encounter in the future.
