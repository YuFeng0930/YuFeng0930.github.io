Singapore Boys' Home

Singapore Boys' Home is a juvenile residential home that provides care and rehabilitation to boys aged seven to sixteen. We went volunteering at Singapore Boy's Home for one year not only to instruct them on their academic work, but also to be their mentor, guiding them along a clear path in their lost life

The majority of my time volunteering there was mentoring an adolescent who was in the fourth grade of secondary school that year. I tutored him on O-level subjects and chatted with him.

Since taking pictures is prohibited in Singapore Boys' Home, we took a group photo outside.
